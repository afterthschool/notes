# Why Do Organisms Reproduce?

Before we discuss the mechanisms by which organisms reproduce, let us ask a more basic question:

## Why do organisms reproduce?

Reproduction is not necessary to maintain the life of an individual organism, unlike essential life processes such as:
- Nutrition  
- Respiration  
- Excretion  

On the other hand:
- Reproduction requires a lot of energy  
- An organism spends energy to create new individuals  

So, why should an individual organism spend energy on a process it does not need to stay alive?  
This is an important question worth discussing.

---

## Observation in Nature

We notice organisms mainly because they reproduce.

- If there were only one non-reproducing organism of a kind:
  - It is unlikely we would notice its existence  
- Large numbers of organisms make a species visible

---

## Identifying a Species

How do we know two organisms belong to the same species?

Usually:
- They **look similar to each other**

Thus:
- Reproduction creates new individuals
- These individuals **resemble their parents**
